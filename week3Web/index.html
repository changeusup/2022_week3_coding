A Typical Day in My Life

(before university)


So what my day looks like.

10 AM

Wake up at around 10 AM because I keep making bad life decisions. I force myself out of bed and brush my teeth in the bathroom. I’m tired so I make instant coffee in the kitchen.


11 AM

I take the bus to my parent’s business to help. Mom is a pharmacist so I help her organize medicine and do payments for customers. Work is boring and repetitive. I go out to eat at a nearby restaurant for lunch at 1 PM. Come back and do more work until around 6 PM.


6 PM

Take the same bus route back home. If I’m feeling hungry, I buy a quick snack at 7-11. I do a bit of exercise at home and then take a shower. Parents come back from work and mom starts making dinner. Meanwhile, I play video games, watch Youtube, or do a drawing in my spare time. Eat dinner at 9 PM. Brush my teeth, do whatever I want and then sleep really late at night.
